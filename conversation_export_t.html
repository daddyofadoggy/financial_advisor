  • Probability Assessment: Capped Upside: High (it's a feature of the strategy). Directional Risk: Medium.
  • Mitigation Strategies:
  1. Strike Selection: Sell calls further out-of-the-money to capture more potential upside, but this will result in lower premiums. This is a direct trade-off to manage.
  2. Rolling: If the stock approaches the strike price, you can "roll" the option to a higher strike price and a later expiration date, allowing more room for the stock to run. This requires active management.
